Reconstruction of weak consonants

Weak consonants have to be written in the cases of infirmae verbs as “jri̯” – “to do”, but not in the case of substantives derived from verbs of ult. Inf. classes as in “wꜣ.t” – “way”.

Nisbe- and Dual-Endings and Double-M17:

Endings of nisba-adjectives and inside a phonetically written dual are represented as “j”.

Only phonemes expressed by a double “M17” are to be written as “y”.
