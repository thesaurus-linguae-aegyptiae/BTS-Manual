Suffixes

Suffixes are to be marked consistently by “=”. Note the white-space between a word and “=”. Lexically bound suffixes have to be marked by “≡” to declare the suffix as included in the word (e.g. personal names).

Examples
Morphological Suffixes:
correct: sḏm =f incorrect: sḏm=f
Lexical Bound Suffixes:
correct: Nṯr-ꜥpr≡f incorrect: Nṯr-ꜥpr =f
