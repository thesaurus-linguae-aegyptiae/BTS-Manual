Quick view

When you have maximized or minimized one window or several of the workspace (Corpus Navigator, Texteditor, etc.), their icons will appear on the right and left side of the User Interface. You can open a quick view of the chosen window (and a chosen tab of that window) by clicking on its icon on the right and left side of the UI.

This example shows the maximized Texteditor. On the left there are two symbols on the right seven:

Images/maximize_Text_Editor_Buttons_markiert.jpg

On mouse-over at a ToolTip "CorpusNavigatorPart" appears. If you click it, the quick view of the Corpus Navigator appears:

Images/maximize_Text_Editor_Corpus_Schnellansicht.jpg

The symbol of the currently chosen quick view is encircled. The quick view is completely functional. To close it, you need to click the symbol again. The entire window remains minimized.
