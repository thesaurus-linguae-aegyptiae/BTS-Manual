Thesaurus Navigator

The "Thesaurus Navigator" is located on the left of your work space in Thesaurus view. It includes key words that you can apply to the texts and objects metadata (passport data).

The toolbar of the "Thesaurus navigator" contains the following entries:

Add Thesaurus Root Entry

This adds a new "Thesaurus Root" entry after the letter "P".

Add Thesaurus Child Entry

This adds a new child entry to a selected root entry or child entry.

This moves an entry from the Thesaurus to Bin.

"Search": Search the Thesaurus for entries.

Bin

Entries deleted in "Thesaurus" appear in "Bin" tab. They are not grouped. Deleting an item here will remove it permanently. Please note that generating the list of the objects in the "Bin" tab may take up to one minute the first time you click it after installing BTS. After this the preview works faster.

Drop-down menu ""

"Delete Permanenty": Delete selected entry completely.

"Edit Updaters/Readers": Assign user roles for thesaurus entries.

"Open Revision History": opens the history of the changes made for the selected lemma

"Filter": Apply different filters on the thesaurus entires:

...by Project

...by Creator

...by Updaters

...by Review Status

...by Visibility

To apply the filter, activate the relevant check boxes
